Ali Mohammed Bazarah, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor of Management Information Systems

Stonehill College

Biography

Dr. Ali Mohammed Bazarah is an assistant professor of management information systems with expertise in data visualization, system analysis and design, and geographic information systems. He is the designer of the "LA Travelers" app, which provides guidance to visitors in Los Angeles.

Before joining Stonehill College, Dr. Bazarah served as an adjunct lecturer at California State University, Long Beach, where he taught undergraduate information systems courses and graduate-level system analysis and design.
